Bangers is a decorative font designed by Vernon Adams between 2007 and 2014 (exact year unknown). It is a font designed to mimic the style of mid-20th century comic books used today by over 140,000 websites. I was originally drawn to this font because of its resemblance to comic books and vibrant style, but I remained interested by its significance to American culture, and to me, it was a good tribute to the deceased creator.
